Expert Tips to Maximize Earnings with Smart Tags
----------

Even with the most advanced artificial intelligence, a human touch is still required to perfect your metadata strategy. Combining smart software with industry best practices is the ultimate recipe for microstock success. Here are actionable tips to elevate your tagging game.

* **Review and Refine AI Suggestions:** Never blindly accept every keyword the AI generates. Skim through the list to ensure all tags accurately represent the primary subject of your media. Remove any terms that feel loosely related.
* **Prioritize Keyword Order:** Many agencies, like Adobe Stock, place heavy emphasis on the first ten keywords. Always drag your most important, descriptive terms to the top of your metadata list before exporting.
* **Blend Literal and Conceptual Tags:** Make sure your final list includes physical descriptions (e.g., "laptop," "coffee," "desk") alongside emotional concepts (e.g., "productivity," "overworked," "success").
* **Use Specific Phrases Over Single Words:** Buyers often search for exact phrases. Ensure your AI tool generates compound tags like "young businesswoman" or "modern home office" rather than just single, isolated words.
* **Analyze High-Selling Competitors:** Look at the top-ranking images in your niche to see what keywords they utilize. Ensure your AI software is capturing those high-value industry terms.
* **Avoid Trademarked Terms:** AI might accurately identify a branded object, like an "iPhone" or "Nike shoes." Be careful to remove these trademarked tags unless you are uploading under an editorial license.
* **Utilize Broad and Niche Tags:** Include broad categories like "technology" to capture general searches, but also include hyper-specific tags like "mechanical keyboard" to capture targeted buyers.

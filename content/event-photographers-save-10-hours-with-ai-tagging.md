* **Review the AI Output:** Always take a quick glance at the generated keywords before exporting. While AI is brilliant, you may want to remove a tag that does not perfectly align with the specific commercial intent you had in mind.
* **Prioritize the First 10 Tags:** Many stock agency algorithms place the highest SEO weight on the first ten keywords attached to your image. Ensure the absolute most descriptive and accurate words are moved to the front of your metadata list.

* **Batch Similar Images Together:** Upload images from the same event in cohesive batches to your AI tool. This allows you to apply bulk conceptual keywords (like "annual conference 2024") to the entire set simultaneously, speeding up the process further.

Expert Tips for AI Keywording Workflows
----------

To truly maximize your earnings and efficiency, you need to use AI tools strategically. Here are actionable tips from top-selling microstock contributors to optimize your workflow:

* **Review Before Exporting:** Always do a quick visual scan of the AI-generated tags. While AI is incredibly accurate, a human touch ensures perfection, especially for highly abstract AI-generated art.
* **Leverage Batch Editing:** If an entire photoshoot features the same model, use batch tools to apply specific demographic tags (e.g., age, ethnicity) to all images simultaneously.
* **Prioritize Order:** Many agencies prioritize the first 10-15 keywords. Ensure your AI tool places the most vital, descriptive terms at the beginning of your keyword list.
* **Utilize Negative Prompts:** When generating metadata for AI art (like Midjourney), ensure you remove prompt commands (like --v 5 or 4k) from your final titles, as agencies dislike technical prompt jargon.
* **Keep Titles Natural:** Ensure your titles read like natural sentences rather than a list of commas. AI tools usually do this by default, but it is worth double-checking.
* **Consistent Routines:** Those who find real stock success streamline image cataloging with ai tags by following a strict daily or weekly routine. Make uploading a habit, not a chore.
